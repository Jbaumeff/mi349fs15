Key Play - Cheer On!

Decription

Key Play allows you to cheer on all your favorites teams. Whether that team is a college team or any professional team in either the MLB, MLS, NBA, NFL, or NHL. Key Play swaps between the selected team's primary colors and displays the team's nickname.

You can select between five different sounds, and three different modes in which those sounds play.

Shake - Just shake your iPhone back and forth to play sounds.
Tap - Tap the screen to play sounds.
Auto - Sounds play automatically at a speed that you choose.

More teams and more features to come!
