Star Hunter

Decription

Star Hunter is a top down shoot 'em up that throws the player into hectic and challenging environments. The player will collect star gems by killing enemies on the planet. After the player kills enough the enemies, the planets god (boss) comes to try and protect the planet. As the player collects star gems of different worth, the player is able to buy new weapons at the star hunters space station. Use different power ups, guns, and abilities to conquer the galaxy. Start hunting!

Star Hunter is a 14 week, student made project at Michigan State University in the Game Design Specialization. Star Hunter features 4 unique levels, a shop to unlock weapons, and roughly 1 hour of game play.
