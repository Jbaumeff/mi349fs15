Solid State

Decription

Solid State is a simple node based RTS (Real-time Strategy) with a heavy emphasis on fast decision making. Take back control of your server as your battle against Rogue data threatens the very existence of the Solid State. Using your tech tree, you can specialize for a play style that suits you. Take back corrupted nodes on diverse play fields that contain buildings that change the very nature of the fight.

Solid State is a 5 week, student made project at Michigan State University. Solid State features 11 Levels and roughly 1 hour of game play.
